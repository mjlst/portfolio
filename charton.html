Charton, Daily Data-visualization

Charton is a ten-seconds-a-day data reviewing device, which will allow users to learn about their important daily choices like budget management and diets. Users can link their mobile application to Charton application to receive a brief review of the data. When they are in front of the device, it detects them and turns on to provide the statistics.

"What if, we can receive a simple review about our daily life?"

Daily Data visualization

Users can choose where to receive the reviews by simply attaching the Charton device on any flat surface. This can be anywhere they pass by daily around their eye level.
