Bookclilp, mobile library
management system

Bookclip is an application that helps students to actively utilize the library by tracking their book checkout history. Also, it gives item recommendations based on their major and search keywords. This information will be connected to the school library management system for further understanding and management for librarians.

A guide for contraception
& birth control

A Guide for Contraception is an application, which provides birth control methods information with illustration. The purpose of the application is to increase accessibility of the information by distributing it through mobile devices and to provide it within clear and efficient visual hierarchy.

Rig-it, Mobile Application for Emergency Medical Technicians

Rig-it mobile application is for making ambulance equipment checks more interactive and creates a positive feedback loop of helpful behavior among Emergency Medical Technicians. The app will ensure accuracy and make a tedious task more fun.
