The Last Egg, Playground game with Playstation Move Controller

The Last Egg is a digital playground game for two teams of 3 players that uses the PlayStation Move Motion Controllers. The game consists of 3 rounds of 1 minute each, in which both teams have to compete to get the last egg : a randomly chosen PS Move with the white light.

Tetronic is a cooperative 4 player Tetris game using Jam O drum platform, a wheel based controller. Rolling the wheel changes the position of the blocks and tapping the wheel changes the shape of the block. The project was done in a week with team of five people.

Maze of Heart

Maze of Heart is physical puzzle game that users will move around their body to collect scattered heart pieces of a robot. There are obstacles and platforms including spring, and elevators.

A unique way of using Microsoft Kinect with your whole body

Maze of Heart consists of a robot that gets its heart broken in multiple pieces. The player’s objective is to take all the heart pieces that are around the robot’s body back to its heart. The player will get help from a mouse, a friend of the robot, by indicating the player in what part of the body the pieces are. This way the player will have to move its body in multiple ways to effectively take the pieces back to the heart.

Entertainment Design | September 2013

Each project is presented in 2 weeks or less during the course Building Virtual Worlds in Carnegie Mellon University. The screen works related to each project was done using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.
