A guide for contraception & Birth control

A Guide for Contraception is an application, which provides birth control methods information with illustration. The purpose of the application is to increase accessibility of the information by distributing it through mobile devices and to provide it within clear and efficient visual hierarchy.

Problem

"I received a paper about birth control from the Health Service Center. I looked over the pages filled with grim images with tiny fonts. I folded it in half and never opened it up again"

"Needs for important knowledge given in comfort"

Features

Contents Screen

Content screen shows a simple description of each method with Illustration, Effectiveness and costs. Right and Left swipe brings another page with additional information.

Main screen & Categories

Three main category screens help users to find proper contraception method. Users have several choices to search like sex, effectiveness and price.

Icons & Information

Possible Risks and Additional Information can be viewed by tapping each icon. Tapped and viewed icons change colors to indicate details that users checked.

Intro Prototyping

A Guide for Birth Control | June 2014

The Illustration and screen works were made with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.
